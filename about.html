Our Story

Born between cultures.

MEHEK is a fragrance house shaped by Moroccan and Pakistani roots, Italian elegance and the creative energy of London.

The Story Behind MEHEK

One identity, shaped across places.

The story begins with Moroccan and Pakistani heritage: cultures rich in scent, ritual, hospitality and memory. Rose, amber, woods, spice and musk became an emotional language long before MEHEK became a fragrance house.

Italy introduced a different sensibility—considered design, quiet elegance and attention to detail. London later brought movement, contrast and reinvention, creating the space in which these influences could become one contemporary identity.

MEHEK was created to hold all of these places together. Heritage, Milano and London are not separate stories, but chapters of the same world: personal, layered and designed to feel timeless.

The Journey

From heritage to fragrance house.

  1. 1993
