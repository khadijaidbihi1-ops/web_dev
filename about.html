Our Story

One identity, shaped across cultures.

MEHEK was born from the meeting of Moroccan and Pakistani heritage, an upbringing influenced by Italian elegance and a creative vision brought to life in London.

Rather than treating these places as separate inspirations, the brand brings them into conversation. Warm woods, rose, saffron and amber meet luminous citrus, soft florals, clean musk and modern urban contrasts. The result is a fragrance world that feels layered, familiar and entirely its own.

Each collection represents a different chapter: Heritage carries memory forward, Milano celebrates considered elegance, and London expresses movement and reinvention.
