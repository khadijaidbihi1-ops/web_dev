Our Story

Fragrance shaped by culture, memory and place.

MEHEK is a contemporary fragrance house born in London from a shared appreciation for scent, craftsmanship and considered design.

Inspired by the sensory richness of Morocco, the artistry of Pakistan and the elegance of modern European living, each creation translates cultural memory into a refined everyday ritual.
