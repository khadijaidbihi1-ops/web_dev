Three places. Three moods. One story.

The World of MEHEK

Each MEHEK collection is a chapter in a journey shaped by memory, movement and belonging. Heritage looks back to the cultures that formed us. Milano celebrates the elegance in which our vision first learned to take shape. London captures the energy of the city that transformed those influences into something new.

Roots, ritual and memory

Heritage Collection

Heritage is where the story begins: in the warmth of family homes, the depth of old traditions and the scents that remain in memory long after a moment has passed.

“Some fragrances are worn. Others become part of your story.”

Inspired by Morocco and Pakistan, the collection brings together saffron, rose, incense, amber, cedar and oud in compositions that feel warm, intimate and timeless.

Atmosphere Warm · Ceremonial · Enveloping
Signature notes Oud · Saffron · Rose · Amber

Elegance in every detail

Milano Collection

Milano celebrates refined expression, precise design and the effortless elegance found in thoughtful details.

“Elegance lives in the details that never need to announce themselves.”

Bright bergamot, iris, delicate florals, cashmere woods and clean musk create polished fragrances inspired by tailoring, marble interiors and modern Italian restraint.

Atmosphere Polished · Luminous · Sophisticated
Signature notes Bergamot · Iris · Peony · Musk

Movement, contrast and reinvention

London Collection

London is the collection of transformation: layered, individual and shaped by a city where cultures and identities continually meet.

“Every city leaves a scent. London leaves a signature.”

Blackcurrant, peony, tobacco, woods, amber and musk move between fresh rain, hidden gardens, warm interiors and midnight streets.

Atmosphere Modern · Magnetic · Unexpected
Signature notes Blackcurrant · Peony · Tobacco · Woods
