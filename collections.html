The World of MEHEK

Each MEHEK collection is a chapter in a journey shaped by memory, movement and belonging. Heritage looks back to the cultures that formed us. Milano celebrates the elegance in which our vision first learned to take shape. London captures the energy of the city that transformed those influences into something new.

01

Roots, ritual and memory

Heritage Collection

Heritage is where the story begins: in the warmth of family homes, the depth of old traditions and the scents that remain in memory long after a moment has passed.

“Some fragrances are worn. Others become part of your story.”

Inspired by the cultural richness of Morocco and Pakistan, the collection brings together the atmosphere of two worlds connected by hospitality, craftsmanship and a shared love of fragrance. Saffron, rose, incense, amber, cedar and oud recall carved wooden doors, embroidered fabrics, evening gatherings and the quiet beauty of rituals passed from one generation to the next.

The compositions are rich without feeling heavy and opulent without losing intimacy. Dark bottles and warm gold details reflect the collection's character: confident, precious and timeless. Heritage is not simply an interpretation of the past. It is a way of carrying that past forward, allowing tradition to become part of a modern identity.

Atmosphere Warm · Ceremonial · Enveloping
Signature notes Oud · Saffron · Rose · Amber

02

Elegance in every detail

Milano Collection

Milano is a tribute to the place where different influences learned to speak the same visual language: refined, expressive and effortlessly composed.

“Elegance lives in the details that never need to announce themselves.”

The collection draws inspiration from Italian tailoring, luminous interiors, polished stone and the rhythm of a city where heritage and innovation exist side by side. Bright bergamot, delicate florals, iris, cashmere woods and clean musk are arranged with precision, creating fragrances that feel elegant from the first impression to the final trace on the skin.

White and gold packaging reflects the clarity and restraint of the collection. Every element is considered, but nothing feels overstated. Milano represents the art of making sophistication appear natural: a perfectly cut jacket, a quiet café in the afternoon, light falling across a marble floor. It is modern luxury expressed through balance rather than excess.

Atmosphere Polished · Luminous · Sophisticated
Signature notes Bergamot · Iris · Peony · Musk

03

Movement, contrast and reinvention

London Collection

London is the collection of transformation: a portrait of a city where cultures meet, identities evolve and every street can reveal a different mood.

“Every city leaves a scent. London leaves a signature.”

Its fragrances move between contrasts. Fresh rain and warm interiors. Hidden gardens and midnight streets. Historic architecture and restless modern energy. Blackcurrant, peony, tobacco, woods, amber and musk create compositions that feel layered and individual, reflecting a city that never asks people to belong in only one way.

The London collection is confident yet adaptable, designed for lives that move from daylight to evening and from familiar spaces to new possibilities. White bottles with black details mirror the city's graphic contrasts. At its heart is the idea that identity is not fixed: it is created through experience, connection and the freedom to become something entirely your own.

Atmosphere Modern · Magnetic · Unexpected
Signature notes Blackcurrant · Peony · Tobacco · Woods
